Rev. Basil Uche Obinozie

THE VISION STATEMENT

Faith Covenant Dominion Centre Incorporated is a vision given to God’s servant Pastor Basil Uche Obinozie on Sunday July 27th 2008. It has the most humble beginning ever one can imagine as God’s servant started without a single broom but just his faith in Christ Jesus who called him into the ministry of deliverance to set the captives free in a city he barely knew anyone where he was sent to called Warri, Delta State, all the way from his faraway home town in Odekpe, Ogbaru LGA, Anambra State, both in Nigeria. He continued ministering like every other preacher does, most times avoiding deliverance but chose to just preach and teach faith, not until on Thursday the 25th day of August 2016 at about 4am in the morning in his vision during his long prayers and fasting that lasted for two years that the Lord did redirect him back to his gift and ministerial calling which is deliverance.

HOW DID IT HAPPEN?

Not long it was his turn and when he came out to minister he just read few portions of the scriptures from the bible and started ministering deliverance and healing as there was a lot of cases there, it happened that as God was manifesting His power through him everybody in that crowd that was applauding the first preachers turned to him including the Pastors that were applauded for earlier also that he became the talk of the town as he was fast having results of healing miracles and casting out of devils in that vision, in fact according to him, he said it is so amazing the way everything happened in that vision that is better seen than imagine.

THE LORD SAID TO HIM!

When he came back from that vision The Holy Spirit said to him to read Luke chapter four, that whole chapter is all about the teachings, preaching, deliverance, healing and miracles that Jesus did, as he began to read he came to verse eighteen especially and there The Holy Spirit said to him; “this is your assignment and the gift I have given to you” don’t copy like others copying, else you become a photocopy and never original. He later prayed and started ministering the way he saw it in that vision till date.

Faith Convenant Dominion Centre Services

Prayers

Prayer is important because it makes us more like Jesus and because it reveals to us the heart and mind of God.

If you ever get to a place in your life where you feel like something is missing, it’s probably a connection problem: you are disconnected from God and too connected to the world. Prayer and fasting can have a powerful impact on setting your life back on track.

Baptism

Baptism marks the beginning of a journey with God which continues for the rest of our lives, the first step in response to God’s love.

Weddings

Churches are special and unique places to get married in - the prayers, promises and the whole service of celebration become part of your marriage, on the day itself and beyond.

Marriage it is a sign not only of the love of the couple for each other but of the love of God for the couple and the love of God for his people.

Funerals

Our church will always be there to help you through one of life’s most difficult times. A FCDC Church funeral is available to everyone, giving support before, during and after the service, for as long as it’s needed.

The Bible says in 2 Corinthians 5:8 that “to be absent from the body is to be present with the Lord.”

Dedication

The dedication service is based upon a Bible text that is in keeping with the occasion. The introductory words of the officiant express gratitude to God. In most cases, thanks is also expressed to the members for their willingness to make sacrifices, thus enabling the church to be built, as well as to all those who worked on its construction.
